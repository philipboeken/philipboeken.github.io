About
Hi, I'm a third year PhD student at the Korteweg-de Vries Institute for Mathematics of the University of Amsterdam, supervised by Joris Mooij. I’m also part of the Mercury Machine Learning Lab, a collaboration between the TU Delft, University of Amsterdam, and Booking.com, where I'm supervised by Onno Zoeter.
I'm broadly interested in statistical methods for drawing causal conclusions from data, and in how causal assumptions and causal reasoning can be leveraged to improve statistical methods.
